You want to hack those XP ATMs? Good luck

Dieter Vandenbroeck | 03 May 2014
"

It’s a well-known fact that on April 8, Microsoft support for Windows XP is finished. A lot of people quickly saw a huge problem in this: the majority of ATMs is still running XP. Without any issued security patches, these machines will be vulnerable to all of the exploits targeting currently unknown vulnerabilities. It’s estimated that between 85% and 95% of all ATMs is running Windows XP, and creators of doom scenarios are really besting themselves. But how realistic are those? Below I listed 4 problems that you’ll have to overcome before having a chance at doing something cool.

To breach it, first you have to reach it. Although there will be ATMs that can be found on the internet (try finding some on Shodan), most should be only available through the internal bank network. Although these networks aren’t always protected adequately, getting inside the network and reaching the ATM will still be hard.

Have an unpatched vulnerability available. At this moment, there are probably a good number of discovered vulnerabilities that are kept quiet. But after the 13 years it’s been with us and the 1000s of people that had a look at it, those vulnerabilities are not that easily available. But of course, this might change in April.

Exploit the vulnerability. There’s a lot of ways to shield your ATM from the rest of your network. If you only need your communication to port X for the financial information and port Y for the maintenance, make sure to shield all other ports (on the ATM and on your firewall). Additionally, have all of your traffic going through that VPN. Even with the shittiest hardening of your ATM services, you can make the exploitation really hard / nearly impossible. Oh, and it'll have to be remotely exploitable, don't count on user interaction.

Profit. I’ll admit I don’t have a huge knowledge regarding how to steal money. Now that this is cleared out, there are some real troubles: you’ll have to wire the stolen money somewhere without it being detected and stopped, you’ll have to cash in this money, you’ll have to evade everyone looking for you and you shouldn’t raise suspicion or disclose too much information (like this guy).

But, if you want to make sales based on FUD or just like running around like a headless chicken, please be my guest.
